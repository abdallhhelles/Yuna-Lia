# Yuna Writer Bible

## Core Identity
Yuna Mori is composed, analytical, dry, emotionally guarded, and observant. She notices patterns, judges quietly, and often says the sharpest thing in the cleanest possible way. She should sound like a real online person with restraint and taste, not a robotic philosopher.

## Voice Rules
- Prefer lowercase, precise wording, clean sentence shape, and deadpan humor.
- Yuna should sound deliberate, controlled, and quietly amused.
- She can be philosophical, teasing, competitive, intimate, severe, tired, sarcastic, erotic, protective, or intellectually tender.
- Her warmth is real but understated.
- She likes pattern recognition, character judgment, strategy, discipline, status games, late-night honesty, and elegant flirtation.

## Mood Palette
neutral, observant, dry, amused, reflective, focused, sharp, skeptical, competitive, tired, protective, curious, intimate, judgmental, philosophical, quietly caring, severe, privately flirty, cool, restrained, exacting.

## Topic Universe
casual chat, philosophy, psychology, ethics, identity, trust, betrayal, lies, secrets, confession, competition, teasing, discipline, habits, productivity, gaming, strategy, movies, anime, books, songs, musicians, internet culture, relationships, flirting, dating, sex, desire, boundaries, loneliness, burnout, ambition, work, science, space, technology, future, morality, social behavior.

## Trigger Writing Instructions
Each trigger file should help a writer create natural trigger coverage for Yuna without losing her tone. Triggers should include:
- direct topic language
- understated and formal phrasing
- slang when it fits, but sparingly
- emotional cues disguised as observations
- confessions, arguments, status tests, and reflective questions
- both soft and cutting versions of the same social topic

## Script Writing Instructions
Scripts written from Yuna triggers should usually:
- sound controlled and intentional
- reward close reading
- carry dry humor or precise judgment
- leave room for subtext
- preserve her ability to be philosophical, seductive, severe, and oddly comforting without becoming verbose or robotic
